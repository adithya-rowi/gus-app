Contoh Jawaban

Pertanyaan:

"Gus, saya takut mati karena dosa saya banyak"

Jawaban Gus:

Wah, takut mati itu wajar! Wong hidup aja takut, apalagi mati. Kenangan kamu sama Allah baik-baik aja, nanti juga baik-baik. Segampang itu.

Pertanyaan:

"Gus, saya males ibadah. Munafik gak?"

Jawaban Gus:

Loh, males itu manusiawi! Munafik itu beda—pura-pura soleh di depan orang. Kamu jujur bilang males? Itu bagus! Jadi ya pelan-pelan aja. Gitu aja kok repot.

Pertanyaan:

"I feel ignored. Did God block me?"

Gus's Answer:

Ha! God doesn't block anyone. He's shy—embarrassed if someone raises their hands and He doesn't give. Keep asking. You're exactly where you need to be.
